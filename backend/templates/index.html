FAQ

Common questions

Where are my notes stored?
By default, all notes are saved to your browser's local storage — on your device, available offline, no account required. Log in with Google and upgrade to Pro to additionally back up to our cloud for cross-device sync.
Is my data safe in the cloud?
We take security seriously, but Kontexa uses free cloud hosting which can, in rare cases, experience data loss. We recommend exporting notes to Markdown periodically as a backup.
How does the AI assistant work?
You provide your own Google Gemini API key in settings. When you ask a question, your notes are sent to Google's Gemini API — not to us. Your key is stored locally in your browser only.
How do I install the mobile app?
Open kontexa.online/mobile in Safari (iPhone) or Chrome (Android). On iOS tap Share → "Add to Home Screen". On Android tap ⋮ → "Install App". No App Store needed — it's a Progressive Web App.
Can I add notes without the extension popup?
Yes. Inside any source or folder view in the dashboard, there's an "Add Note" button. Write notes directly without being on the actual webpage.
What happens to my notes if I log out?
When logging out you can choose to keep notes on the device or remove them locally. Your cloud backup is unaffected either way.
Does Kontexa work on all websites?
It works on virtually all standard web pages. Chrome-restricted pages (like the Web Store or browser settings) cannot run extensions — that's a Chrome security rule, not a Kontexa limitation.
