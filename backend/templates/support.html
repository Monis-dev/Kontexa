Kontexa is a Chrome extension that lets you save notes and annotations directly on any webpage. Your notes are attached to the exact URL you saved them on, so they reappear every time you revisit that page.
Sign in with your Google account inside the extension, then click Account → Sync. Your notes will be backed up to our server and restored on any device where you sign in with the same account. Sync requires a Pro subscription.
The free plan lets you create and manage notes locally on your current device. Notes are stored in your browser and are available offline. Cloud sync, cross-device access, and advanced features require a Pro upgrade.
Local notes are stored in your browser's storage — reinstalling the extension clears this. If you had a Pro account with sync enabled, sign back in and tap Sync to restore your notes from the cloud. We strongly recommend enabling sync to avoid data loss.
Subscriptions are processed via Razorpay. To cancel, contact us using the form on this page with your email address and the subject "Cancel Subscription" — we will cancel it and confirm within 24 hours. You will retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period.
