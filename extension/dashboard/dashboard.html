How ContextNote Stores Your Data

🏠 Local by Default (Offline) If you don't log in, your notes live entirely on your computer's hard drive. They are fast, completely private, and available offline.
☁️ Cloud Sync (Cross-Device) Logging in backs up your notes to our cloud servers so you can view them on any device.
⚠️ Important Warning ContextNote uses free cloud hosting. In rare cases, cloud backups might be lost. Your local device is your safest primary backup!
